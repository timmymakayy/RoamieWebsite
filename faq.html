Login to Your Account: Visit our website and log in to your account using the credentials you used when making the booking. Navigate to Booking History: Once logged in, look for a section on the website labeled "My Account" or similar. Within this section, you should find an option like "Booking History" or "My Bookings." Locate Your Reservation: In the Booking History, you'll see a list of your reservations. Locate the specific booking for which you need the confirmation and itinerary. Access Details: Click on the booking to access the details. Here, you should find links or buttons to view and download your booking confirmation and itinerary. Download or Print: Depending on the platform, you may have the option to download or print your confirmation and itinerary for your records.
